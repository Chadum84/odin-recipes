Lasagna

Delicious lasagna recipe. Yum!

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian Sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves or garlic.
  • Tomato: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoos of white sugar.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the Sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
