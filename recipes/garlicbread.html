Garlic Bread

Easy roasted garlic bread made with roasted garlic, butter, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 3 heads garlic
  • 2 tablespoods olive oil
  • 1 (1 pound) loaf Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsely (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Slive the tops of garlic heads, so the tip of each clove is exposed. Place garlic heads, cut-sides-up, on a baking sheet; drizzle tops with olive oil.
  3. Set the ove to broil.
  4. Slive bread loaf in half horizontally; place cut-side up on a baking sheet.
  5. Squeeze garlic cloves from their skins into a medium bowl. Stir in butter, parmesan cheese, and parsley until well blended. Spread onto the cut sides of bread.
  6. Broil bread until toasted. About 5 minutes.
